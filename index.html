Spireguard of the Spirelands

The Spireguard are a formidable and disciplined group with a culture deeply rooted in tradition and order, with society revolving around the protection and reverence of the Spire, a colossal structure surpassing the surrounding mountains in height.

The Sun Chasers of Quentin

The Sun Chasers are a serene and enlightened society renowned for their devotion to balance and aesthetic grace. Their civilization is intricately woven with a philosophy of tranquility and wisdom, with magical and societal emphasis on harmony with the natural world.

Arcane Order of Starborne

The Arcane Order is a mystic and enigmatic society, originating from the depths of the cosmos. Their civilization is deeply rooted in pursuit of cosmic harmony and the protection of Eturnos from interstellar threats.

The Enclave

The Enclave is a secluded region characterized by its challenging geography and geothermal activity. The Enclave - also the name of the people who settled there - are secluded and isolationist, and have mastered the dangerous terrain through the construction of Heralds.
